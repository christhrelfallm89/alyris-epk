Bio

ALYRIS are a modern metal band blending Metalcore intensity with Alternative Metal weight and a sharp, contemporary edge. Built for impact, their sound pairs aggressive riffs and tight low-end with dynamic vocals and cinematic tension.

Their EP Envy & Demise showcases that range — with standout track “Tempest” earning praise from BSBTV: “This to me was the most unexpected and incredible track! The mix, the video, the vocals, the guitar work, the bass - there is nothing I would change, it's Flawless!”
